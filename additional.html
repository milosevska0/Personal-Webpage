My Interests

I enjoy reading psychology and philosophy books, self-help books and classics. One of my favorite books is Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind, by Yuval Noah. In the books the author talks about our species, starting the story from the beginning of the human era, developing the story to the modern day Homo Sapiens. Additionally, i really enjoyed Dostoevsky's Crime and Punishment, George Orwell's Animal Farm and 1984, A Mind For Numbers by Barbara Oakley and The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck, by Mark Manson. In terms of movies, some of my favorites are: The Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind, Once Upon a Time in... Hollywood, Inglorious Basterds, Pulp Fiction, Inception... Speaking of music, I have a versatile style and genre that changes over time. Genres that tend to stay with me are Alternative Rock Nu Rock, Progressive Rock and Indie.
